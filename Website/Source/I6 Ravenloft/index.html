I6: Ravenloft

1st Ed


Name: I6: Ravenloft
Media Type(s): Module
Edition: 1st Ed
Release Date: 01/11/1983
Extra Info:
 Authors: Tracy Hickman and Laura Hickman
 Editor: Curtis Smith
 Graphic Designer: Debra Stubbe
 Illustrator: Clyde Caldwell
 Module Info: An adventure for 6-8 characters of levels 5-7
 Personal Notes: The term 'Vistani' had not been coined yet, instead the term 'gypsy' was used in this book. I've used 'Vistani' in its place.
